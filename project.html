About:

Welcome to the Pokédex. This app is built with a Javascript frontend and a Rails API backend. This web application will alow you to keep track of your own Pokémon. You will also be able to make comments for any Pokémon of your choosing! Gotta Catch Them All!

Click here for the repo!

Upon launching the application, you will be greeted by a main welcome page and you will also see the text boxes in which you can input your information for your Pokémon. You can make a Pokémon and input a name, move and ability. You can also make a comment for a Pokémon of your choosing. You also have the abilities to edit or delete a Pokémon or comment.
